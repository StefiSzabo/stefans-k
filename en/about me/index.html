My personality

I am Aquarius in the Western Zodiac and my personality, according to the MBTI test, is INFJ-A.

The result INFJ-A of an MBTI test means that that person have the personality type of an Advocate, with the Assertive variant.
According to the 16Personalities website, an Advocate is someone with the Introverted, Intuitive, Feeling, and Judging personality traits.
They tend to approach life with deep thoughtfulness and imagination. Their inner vision, personal values, and a quiet, principled version of humanism guide them in all things.
The Assertive variant indicates that that person is confident, self-assured, and resistant to stress.
That person is likely to have a strong sense of self and his/her own abilities, and he/she doesn't let doubts or challenges stop him/her from pursuing his/her goals.
That person is also less likely to worry about what others think of him/her, or to compare himself/herself to others.
INFJ-A is one of the rarest personality types in the world, making up less than 1% of the population.
That person is part of a group of people who are idealistic, creative, compassionate, and driven by a desire to make a positive difference in the world.
Some famous examples of INFJ-A personalities are Nelson Mandela, Martin Luther King Jr., Mother Teresa, and Oprah Winfrey.
