The Community Guidelines

Welcome everyone to Ștefan's K.

I want my blog to be a safe, welcoming, and respectful place where everyone feels like they can trust this blog and the people they meet here.

So, to achieve this, I needed to set up a few guidelines, especially for the comments sections.

You can report any violations, firstly, by flagging the comments or marking them as spam, and secondly, by contacting me through any of these channels.
When contacting me about these violations, you must to include a screenshot of the comments, a link to the blog's page where it appears, and the violator's nickname.

I may edit those comments to follow these guidelines, or altogether remove them.
Repeated violators will also face a suspension or ban.

Here are the guidelines:

1. Show kindness and respect

Treat others with the same level of respect and kindness that you expect in return.
Remember that there are people behind the screen and your words affect them.
I do not tolerate any kind of harassment, hate speech, discrimination, or bullying.

2. Keep it appropriate

Please refrain from sharing vulgar, offensive, or inappropriate content.
This includes nudity, graphic violence, or explicit language.

3. No spamming, irrelevant content, or self-promotion

I do not allow spam or irrelevant content.
Self-promotion is permitted only if the promotion content resonates with the page where it appears.

4. Protect privacy

Please do not share any personal information about others without their consent. This includes contact information, addresses, or any other sensitive data.

5. Follow the law

I expect everyone to follow the law and not engage in illegal activities or encourage others to do so.

6. Report violations

If you witness or see any violations of these guidelines, please report them at once.

In a few words, "Be polite about the community and the law.".

By following these guidelines, we can create a positive and engaging community where everyone feels welcome and valued. Thank you for your cooperation.
